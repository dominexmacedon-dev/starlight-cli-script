Dominex Macedon

Developer • Language Creator • Interpreter Enthusiast

Introduction

Dominex Macedon, also known as Nyi Nyi Linn Htet, is a developer focused on programming language design, interpreter architecture, and building systems that help others understand how code works internally.

Background

Born on March 21, 2006, Dominex began his journey with curiosity about how software systems operate behind the scenes. Growing up in a hardworking family, he developed a mindset centered on persistence and continuous learning.

His father, U Kyaw Moe, works as a carpenter and construction worker, demonstrating discipline and resilience. His mother, Daw Thet Thet Maw, supports the family with care and dedication.
